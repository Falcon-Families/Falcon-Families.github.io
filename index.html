Falcon Families

of Sheboygan Falls Elementary School

Supporting a place to learn, a place to feel safe, and a place to explore and build strong academic skills.

 

Serving the students of SFES

  • Field trips

    We make more field trips possible to more enriching destinations.

  • Extra classroom supplies

    Whether art supplies or headphones, we help teachers have what they need to inspire their class.

  • Teacher appreciation

    We provide special thank yous to help keep our teachers motivated do pour themselves into their students.

  • Artist in residence

    We support the music program's artist in residence program that brings in musical talent specially geared to budding musicians.

Get involved

We bring together our community though various fundraisers. All proceeds benefit the students of SFES. We need volunteers to help with fundraisers and to lead the organization. To learn how you can help, come to the next meeting on our calendar or email us.
